Pineapple is known scientifically Ananascomosus Merr.
Its a member of the Bromeliaceae family. 
Pineapple is known to be a native to South America and believed to be brought to Europe by Spanish explorers. 
Plant of the pineapple is also grown indoors or out as an ornamental plant. 
Citric and malic acids are found in good quantities in pineapple fruits. 
Citric acid concentrations in some cultivars may exceed 8%. 
Ascorbic acid is also found in moderate amounts in pineapple fruit.
